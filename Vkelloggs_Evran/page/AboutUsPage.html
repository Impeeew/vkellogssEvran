What Is VKellogg's

vkellogs is a leading cereal brand in Indonesia. First founded in Bekasi in 2023, currently vkellogs has more than 199 stores in big cities throughout Indonesia. vkellogs provides high quality cereal products at affordable prices. vkellogs also has a team of trained nutritionists who regularly take refresher courses to learn the latest nutrition on the market and stay up to date with the latest medical changes.
